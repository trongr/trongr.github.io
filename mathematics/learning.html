How to take notes

Making summaries

Make summaries of the main themes and the story so far, every time you finish an idea, a theorem, proof, or an example, etc. When you start a new idea, go over your summary from before. Sort of like recaps in long running TV shows---"Previously on Lost...." That's one way to make things make sense. Ideas are like wheat at harvest: there're too many, and you can comprehend them only by bundling them all up together. [ WOW I'm doing metaphors now. ]
