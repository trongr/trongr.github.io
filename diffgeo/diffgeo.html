Initial D

Tangent Vector

Proposition. If the tangent vector of a parametrised curve is constant, then the image of the curve is part of a straight line.

Exercise. $\gamma(t) = (t^2, t^4)$ is not a parametrisation of the parabola $y = x^2$ because $t^2$ is always positive, so the negative half of the parabola isn't parametrised.

Exercise. Find a parametrisation of the level curve $y^2 - x^2 = 1.$

This is the hyperbola with parametrisation $ \gamma(t) = (\sinh t, \cosh t), $ since $$ \cosh^2 t - \sinh t = 1. $$

Exercise. Find a parametrisation of the level curve $ \frac{x^2}{4} + \frac{y^2}{9} = 1. $

This is an ellipse with parametrisation $ \gamma(t) = (2\cos t, 3\sin t). $
